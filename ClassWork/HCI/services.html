Frequently Asked Questions

If your waste wasn't collected on the scheduled day, please report it through our i-Clean Selangor app, call our hotline at 1800 88 2824, or submit a report on our website. Our team will investigate and arrange for collection within 24 hours.

You can request bulk waste pickup online through our website or by calling our hotline. Provide details about the items, and we'll give you a quote and schedule a convenient pickup time.

We don't collect hazardous materials (chemicals, batteries, medical waste), liquid waste, or certain construction materials. Please contact us for guidance on proper disposal of specialized items.

Download the i-Clean Selangor app to track collection trucks in real-time using GPS. You'll receive notifications when the truck is approaching your area.
