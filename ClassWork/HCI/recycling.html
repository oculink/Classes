What Can You Recycle?

Learn about materials we accept and how to prepare them

📄

Paper & Cardboard

Accepted Items:

  • Newspapers & magazines
  • Office paper & documents
  • Cardboard boxes
  • Paper bags
  • Telephone directories

Preparation Tips:

  • Remove plastic wrapping
  • Flatten cardboard boxes
  • Keep clean and dry
🍶

Plastic

Accepted Items:

  • PET bottles (Type 1)
  • HDPE containers (Type 2)
  • PP containers (Type 5)
  • Plastic bags (clean)

Preparation Tips:

  • Rinse containers
  • Remove caps and labels
  • Check recycling number
🥫

Metal

Accepted Items:

  • Aluminum cans
  • Steel cans
  • Metal containers
  • Aluminum foil

Preparation Tips:

  • Rinse thoroughly
  • Remove labels if possible
  • No need to crush cans
🍾

Glass

Accepted Items:

  • Glass bottles (all colors)
  • Glass jars
  • Glass containers

Preparation Tips:

  • Rinse and clean
  • Remove caps and lids
  • Separate by color if possible

❌ Items NOT Accepted

Food-contaminated materials Styrofoam Plastic bags (dirty) Ceramics & pottery Window glass Light bulbs Medical waste Batteries (separate collection)
