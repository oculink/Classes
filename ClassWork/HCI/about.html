About KDEBWM

Malaysia's Smart Waste Management Company of the Year

Who We Are

KDEB Waste Management Sdn Bhd (KDEBWM) was appointed by the State Government of Selangor to provide comprehensive waste management services across 12 municipal councils. Since 2017, we've been committed to keeping Selangor clean, green, and sustainable.

With over 3,000 dedicated employees, 1,200+ fleet vehicles, and state-of-the-art technology, we collect and manage 3,500 tons of waste daily, ensuring efficient service delivery to over 2 million residents.

🎯 Our Vision

To be a prominent waste management company committed to promoting a cleaner and greener environment for future generations.

💡 Our Mission

To provide efficient, reliable, and environmentally responsible waste management services through innovation and technology.

Our Journey

Milestones in building Selangor's waste management infrastructure

2017

Company Establishment

KDEBWM appointed by Selangor State Government. Began operations in 3 municipal councils.

2018

Expansion Phase

Expanded to 8 councils. Launched i-Clean Selangor mobile app for citizen engagement.

2020

Full Coverage

Achieved complete coverage of all 12 municipal councils in Selangor. Fleet expanded to 1,000+ vehicles.

2022

Waste-to-Energy

Launched Sultan Idris Shah Green Energy Plant. 30 MW capacity serving 120,000 homes.

2024

Smart Technology

Implemented GPS tracking, AI route optimization, and Centralized Command Centre.

2026

Excellence Award

Recognized as Malaysia's Smart Waste Management Company of the Year.
